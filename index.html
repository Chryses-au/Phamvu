Resume

Professional experience in medical device development

Bryant is a medical device engineer specializing in taking products from initial concept through full-scale production. With experience spanning startups and established medical technology companies, he brings a hands-on, end-to-end approach to device development — from requirements gathering and feasibility studies through verification, validation, and design transfer.

His work spans orthopedics, dental implants, surgical robotics, and minimally invasive oncology. Bryant has contributed to multiple issued and pending patents, reflecting his drive to innovate within the constraints of regulatory and manufacturing requirements.

A background of a CNC machinist that is a Mechanical Engineer with 10+ years of experience developing electromechanical, robotic, and minimally invasive medical systems. Skilled in precision mechanisms, thermal systems, high‑pressure gas flow, sensor integration, DFM/DFA, GD&T, dFMEA, root-cause/corrective actions, and V&V within ISO 13485 and Class II environments.
